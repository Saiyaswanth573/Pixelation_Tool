Guess the Pixelated Image

A fun and challenging pixelation quiz.

Rules & Scoring

  • Total of 15 questions in the round.
  • Each image starts at a high pixelation level.
  • Players have 30 seconds to buzz in.
  • If both teams fail to answer, the pixelation is reduced.
  • 10 Points: Level 3 pixelated
  • 8 Points: Level 2 pixelated
  • 5 Points: Level 1 pixelated
